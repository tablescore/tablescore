About tablescore

All restaurants. All reviews. One search.

New York City has tens of thousands of restaurants and no shortage of expert opinion about where to eat. The problem has never been a shortage of information — it's that all of it lives in completely separate places, with no way to search across it at once.

tablescore is the missing layer. It brings every major NYC restaurant together with expert reviews from The Infatuation, Michelin, the New York Times, Eater, Grub Street, and more — and makes all of it searchable and filterable in one place. Want to know which highly-rated restaurants deliver on DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub, or Seamless? That's one search. tablescore gives you a complete picture of any restaurant in the city, instantly.

We built tablescore because finding a great restaurant shouldn't require a research project.

Our Data

tablescore aggregates publicly available review data from New York City's most trusted food publications and platforms, including The Infatuation, Michelin Guide, The New York Times, Eater, Beli, Grub Street, New York Magazine, and One Bite. We do not own or control the content published by these sources. All reviews and ratings remain the intellectual property of their respective publishers.
